About

Building what matters.

Mohamed Elsafi Abd Elhalim is a PhD-holding entrepreneur and the CEO & Founder of Alsharq Alawsat Labs — a forward-thinking innovation lab based in Alexandria, Egypt.

With a relentless focus on building businesses that lead and create lasting impact, Mohamed brings academic rigor and executive vision to every venture he leads.

His work sits at the intersection of technology, business strategy, and regional development — driving the next generation of solutions across the Middle East.
